Why This Website Exists

This website was created as a voice in the silence.

During my novitiate year, my phone will be taken away. I will be immersed in prayer, silence, and formation. But I did not want the talks on the Holy Face of Jesus to stop.

So I built this — a place where souls can come to encounter the Holy Face. A place where the love of Jesus continues to speak, even when I cannot.

Each talk is a prayer. Each word is an offering. May the Holy Face of Jesus bless you, heal you, and draw you closer to His Sacred Heart.

"In the silence, He speaks."

— Mac-Collins Ukwuoma · Novice (God's Grace)
