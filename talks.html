Talks on the Holy Face

The Face of Mercy

A reflection on the Holy Face of Jesus · 5 min read

The face of Jesus is the face of mercy. When we look upon His Holy Face, we see the love of the Father made visible. In every wound, there is healing. In every tear, there is hope.

Silence That Speaks

Finding God in the stillness · 4 min read

Silence is not empty. It is full of God. When we stop speaking, we begin to hear. The Holy Face speaks most clearly in the quiet heart.

The Holy Face and the Poor

Seeing Christ in the suffering · 6 min read

Every suffering face is a reflection of the Holy Face. When we serve the poor, we touch the face of Jesus. This is the heart of the Gospel.

Behold the Man

A meditation on the Passion · 5 min read

Ecce Homo. Behold the Man. The Holy Face crowned with thorns is the glory of God hidden in suffering. In His wounds, we are healed.

The Holy Face in the Eucharist

Encountering Christ in the Blessed Sacrament · 5 min read

The same face that hung on the cross is present in the Eucharist. When we adore, we look upon His Face. When we receive, we become His face to the world.
